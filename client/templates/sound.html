Recent Work

The Woodsman by James Ortiz



Directed by James Ortiz, Presented by Strangemen and Co.
New World Stages
February 2016

Work for The Woodsman included an original thirty minute ambient soundscape emulating the mythical forests of Oz. Click the thumbnail to view a screenshot of the recording session featuring 17 separate tracks including 8 original field recordings and detailed automation curves. Examples of specific mythical creatures below.

Below are a collection of mythical animal noises created to represent the various wildlife encountered in the land of Oz.

The Blue-Footed Grackle

The Carnivorous Treehog

The Fan-Tailed Yapper

Large-Toothed Howler Ox

Eastern Kalida


Additionally, I developed a custom iOS app for remote control of audio cues during preshow. More detail available here: https://github.com/arsone/thewoodsmanapp, or for the technically inclined, feel free to clone the repo here: https://github.com/arsone/thewoodsmanapp.git
